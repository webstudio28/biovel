# Homepage Structure Plan

This document outlines the proposed structure and ad copy for the homepage of the Vega Test website.

## Hero Section Ad Copy

The hero section copy is designed to be encompassing, combining all aspects of the business into a single, powerful message.

- **Headline:** `Открийте пътя към Вашето оптимално здраве` (Discover the path to your optimal health)
- **Sub-headline:** `Цялостен подход към храненето и здравето, воден от Елена Христова. Защото Вашето тяло заслужава персонална грижа.` (A holistic approach to nutrition and health, guided by Elena Hristova. Because your body deserves personal care.)
- **Call to Action Button:** `Запазете час за консултация` (Book a consultation)

---

## Proposed Home Page Section Order

This structure is designed to tell a compelling story, guiding the visitor from initial interest to becoming a client.

1.  **Hero Section:** 
    - Captivating visual and the core ad copy as described above.

2.  **About Elena Hristova:**
    - Introduce the expert behind the practice to build immediate trust.
    - Highlight her 9+ years of experience and certifications (НУТРИЦИОЛОГИЯ, НАПО).
    - Include a professional, welcoming photo.

3.  **My Philosophy:**
    - A brief, personal section from Elena about her approach to health.
    - Feature the quote: `"Вярвам, че здравето е инвестиция, не разход."` (I believe that health is an investment, not an expense).
    - This section should convey her passion and genuine care.

4.  **Services:**
    - A clear, well-organized overview of the core offerings.
    - Suggested breakdown:
        - **Diagnostics:** Vega Test & Bioscanner.
        - **Personalized Plans:** Nutrition & Detox Programs (including Lymphatic Drainage and Anti-Parasite Programs).
        - **Holistic Therapies:** Australian Bush Flower Essences.

5.  **How It Works (The Process):**
    - Simplify the client journey to make it feel accessible and straightforward.
    - Example steps:
        - **Step 1: Първоначална консултация** (Initial Consultation)
        - **Step 2: Диагностика и анализ** (Diagnostics & Analysis)
        - **Step 3: Изготвяне на персонален план** (Creating a Personal Plan)

6.  **Products:**
    - A dedicated area to feature related products.
    - Mention the `BIO VEL` nutritional supplements.
    - Briefly describe the benefits and how they complement the services.

7.  **Testimonials:**
    - Feature success stories and quotes from satisfied clients.
    - This section provides powerful social proof and builds credibility.

8.  **Call to Action (CTA):**
    - A final, visually distinct section to encourage action.
    - Repeat the primary call to action: "Book a consultation."
    - Include the phone number for direct contact.

9. **Contact & Location:**
